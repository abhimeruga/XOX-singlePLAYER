How to Play | XOX'R

1. The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.

2. You have 3 X's and your opponent has 3 O's.

3. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.

4. The first player to get 3 of her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.

5. When you finish marking all 3 X's, you will be able to move one of the marking(X) to the next cell(top, left, bottom, right).

6. the game continues untill one of them has all thier markings(X/O) in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally).
